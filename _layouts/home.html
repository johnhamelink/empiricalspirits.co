Empirical Spirits is about swerving the categories

Choosing heirloom grains, koji and bespoke yeasts to ferment in-house, our one-off spirits reflect the time and place they came from: a product made in spring might taste different to one made in the fall. To help us highlight those differences, we have designed and built equipment that allows us to distill at 10°C, rather than boiling all the flavour away
